# U.S. Electric Vehicle Market Share Analysis

Analysis of electric vehicle adoption trends across all 50 U.S. states, examining how EVs compare to traditional and alternative fuels and where infrastructure investment should be prioritised.

## Business Context
A government transportation board needs a data-driven report on the current state of alternative fuel vehicles to plan future charging infrastructure and forecast EV adoption.

## Questions Answered
- What percentage of vehicles in each state are EVs, PHEVs, HEVs, and gasoline?
- Which states have the highest EV adoption rates?
- Which alternative fuels are significant vs. niche?
- Where should policymakers prioritise EV infrastructure investment?

## Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- SQL
- Tableau

## Key Findings
- EVs represent a small but fast-growing share of the national fleet
- California leads in absolute EV count; Washington leads in adoption rate
- Ethanol/Flex (E85) is the only alternative fuel with meaningful market presence
- States with high EV adoption but low alternative fuel infrastructure are prime targets for charging network rollout
